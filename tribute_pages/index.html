Mustafa Kemal ATATÜRK

Infinite breath handed down from generation to generation.

That great person was the greatest leader not only for Turkey but also for all eastern nations.

Here's a Time Line of Mustafa Kemal ATATÜRK's Life:

  • 1881 - The birth of Mustafa Kemal in Thessaloniki.
  • 1887 - Mustafa Kemal started his primary education. (He attended the neighborhood school for a short time, then transferred to Şemsi Efendi School, which provides contemporary education, and finished primary school here.)
  • 1893 - The death of Mustafa Kemal's father, Ali Rıza Efendi.
  • 1894 - Mustafa Kemal's entry into Thessaloniki Military High School. (Although he attended the Thessaloniki Civil High School for a while after Şemsi Efendi Primary School, he left this school after his mathematics teacher named Kaymak Hafız unjustly hit him with a stick and applied to the Military High School with his own will and decision and continued his education there.)
  • 1896 - Mustafa Kemal's entry into the Manastır Military High School.
  • 1899 - March 13, 1899: Mustafa Kemal enters the Military Academy in Istanbul.
  • 1902 - February 10, 1902: Mustafa Kemal enters the Military Academy with the rank of lieutenant.
  • 1903 - Mustafa Kemal's transition to the second year of the Military Academy and becoming a first lieutenant
